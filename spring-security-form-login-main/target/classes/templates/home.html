The graphic and typographic designers are well aware of this, and in fact, professionals across the communication industry have a close relationship with these words. So, what exactly is Lorem ipsum? It is a dummy text that lacks any coherent meaning. It consists of a sequence of Latin words that, when arranged, do not form complete sentences, but are used to create a test text that can fill spaces that will later be filled with specific texts composed by communication professionals. It is undoubtedly the most widely used placeholder text, although there are various versions of it that differ based on the order in which the Latin words are repeated.
